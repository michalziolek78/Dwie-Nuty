Why (dwie nuty) two notes?
   They symbolise two different forces balanced out each other, in a space of electronic sounds. On the one hand, trance emanating with magic of rhythm and variety of colourful sounds, on the other hand empathic stream of progressive, which as if calming and body swinging warm wind, caress our consciousness and it’s provide us with pulse for root tribal and timeless electro. In my case, these powers has materialised as two musical projects, which I would like to present you here. They are reflection of my passions and my musical interest. First of them Extreme Discotheque, represent Psychedelic trance genre with variety of it's kinds. While second one Secret Energy represent above-mentioned electro, tech-house, progressive, tribal and techno. On the sites of these projects (menu at the bottom), you will find information concerning the projects, you will listen to my music, and if you find it interesting for you, you will download it for free in mp3 format.
Enjoy and be my guest!
